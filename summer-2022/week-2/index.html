Ogilvy & Mather reports on its first 1⁄25 of a century in Chicago

Four years ago this November, Ogilvy & Mather-Chicago opened its doors. For 1980 our billings will be nearly $80,000,000, seventh among Chicago agencies. We're delighted—and we hope our clients are too, including the twelve who hired us since we got here.

Ogilvy & Mather came to Chicago, like so many companies over the decades, with high hopes, big plans, and a considerable respect for our competition.

How right we were—particularly on that last point. One of the pleasures of working in advertising in Chicago is the tremendous professionalism you find everywhere you look.
